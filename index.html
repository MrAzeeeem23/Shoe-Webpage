About Us: Beat X - Where Comfort Meets Style with a Conscience

At Beat X, we are not just a shoe brand; we are a movement that combines innovation, comfort, , and environmental responsibility to create a truly unique and sustainable footwear experience.

Our Founders:

Founded by Azeem Khan, Prabhat Yadav and Yusuf Timol, Beat X was born out of a shared passion for crafting exceptional footwear. Azeem and Prabhat bring a wealth of experience, expertise, and a vision to revolutionize the footwear industry.

Comfort & Style:

We understand that comfort and style are the cornerstones of every great shoe. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to designing footwear that marries the two seamlessly. Our shoes are meticulously crafted to ensure that every step you take feels like a dream while making a fashion statement.
